How I Felt About This Course

Overall, I found this course to be incrediblly fun. Pairing this with my User Interface course that I'm also taking this semester has really helped me understand some of the ins and outs of web design and has made me a better programmer for it. If I had one complaint to make, it would be that IED modules from the book are a little too particular. In some instances, I found it hard to see where I went wrong in the step that the module said was wrong. Turns out the issue was caused from a previous step that the system had marked correct.

While future web design courses aren't in my graduation requirements, I think I'll be looking for other ways to continue my learnings. I hope by the end of next year, I'll have a few personal sites designed myself.
